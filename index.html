Our Course Structure

Beginner: SAP FI & CO Fundamentals

  • ERP & SAP Overview
  • Navigation (SAP GUI / Fiori)
  • Organizational Structure
  • General Ledger Basics
  • Accounts Payable / Receivable Basics
  • Cost Centers & Profit Centers Overview
  • Simple Postings & Reports
  • Real-world Use Case: Practical exercises on basic postings and navigating through SAP GUI.

Intermediate: GL, AP/AR, Asset Accounting

  • General Ledger Configuration
  • Accounts Payable & Vendor Master
  • Accounts Receivable & Customer Master
  • Tax Configuration
  • Asset Accounting Basics
  • Cost Center & Internal Order Config
  • FI-MM / FI-SD Integration
  • Hands-on Labs: Configure GL, Tax, and AP/AR for a simulated business.

Advanced: S/4HANA FICO

  • New GL Accounting & Parallel Ledgers
  • Foreign Currency Valuation
  • Profitability Analysis (COPA)
  • Product Costing
  • Universal Journal (ACDOCA)
  • SAP Fiori Reporting
  • End-to-End Project Implementation
  • Project Management: Full-cycle project implementation for a real-world business scenario, including reporting and system analysis.
